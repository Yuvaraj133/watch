About Me

I'm TimeWise Carewatch and I am a

The wristwatch-based assistance device is a compact yet powerful solution designed specifically for the needs of elderly individuals. With its wearable design, it stays within close reach at all times, offering a seamless experience for users. Its integrated pulse sensor eliminates the need for physical button presses, automatically detecting irregular heart rates and summoning medical assistance when necessary. Equipped with surveillance features including a camera and audio capabilities, it provides an extra layer of security by allowing remote monitoring of the user's surroundings. Real-time data, including audio, video, and heart rate information, is seamlessly transmitted to a web platform and Android application, enabling caregivers and family members to stay updated on the user's well-being. Additionally, the device streamlines the process of ordering and delivering medications from nearby pharmacies, ensuring timely access to essential drugs. Its remote controllability facilitates easy maintenance and future upgrades, ensuring the device remains relevant and functional over time. Overall, this wristwatch-based assistance device offers a holistic solution to support the independence and safety of elderly individuals, while providing peace of mind to their caregivers and loved ones.
